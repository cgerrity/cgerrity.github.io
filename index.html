About Me

Hello! I'm Charles Gerrity

Welcome to my personal website. I'm a neuroscientist and machine learning engineer specializing in brain-computer interfaces and deep learning applications in neuroscience.

I recently completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Vanderbilt University and am currently a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Department of Psychology. My research focuses on developing deep learning models for neural data analysis and cognitive brain-computer interfaces.

I specialize in processing large-scale neuronal datasets (over 2 TB) using advanced deep learning architectures including variational autoencoders and recurrent networks for decoding choice and decision-making processes. My work bridges the gap between invasive neural recordings, signal processing, data visualization, model design, and decoder analysis.

With expertise spanning from high-density electrophysiology recordings to machine learning pipeline development, I'm passionate about advancing our understanding of the brain and developing technologies that can help decode and understand cognitive processes.
