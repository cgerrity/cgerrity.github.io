# Research & Engineering Projects

### Multi-Task Neural Decoding Architectures
I engineered a custom Multi-Task Gated Recurrent Variational Autoencoder (MT-GR-VAE) to process high-density neural recordings.
* **Performance:** Achieved 80% accuracy on reward-associated predictions and 62% balanced accuracy across multi-label classes.
* **Theory & Implementation:** Utilized adaptive loss normalization to decode cognitive states from the Prefrontal Cortex and Anterior Cingulate Cortex.
* **Explainable AI:** Identified novel brain region contributions to learning using XAI analysis to interpret the neural network's internal decoding mechanisms.

### Scalable Neural Processing & Pipeline Optimization
I established a high-performance signal chain for acute high-density electrophysiology (300+ channels) integrating NeuroNexus probes and Intan controllers.
* **Pipeline Engineering:** Built a scalable MATLAB-based data processing pipeline that has successfully processed over 4 TB of massive neurophysiological data.
* **Speed Optimization:** Optimized the deep learning training pipeline, resulting in a **4x increase in training speed** compared to the prior system.
* **Deployment:** This central repository is actively utilized across the lab to standardize and accelerate analysis for multiple team members.

### Technical Leadership & Mentorship
A core component of my work involves bridging the gap between engineering theory and practical research execution for interdisciplinary teams.
* **Mentorship:** Mentored graduate and undergraduate students on machine learning projects, directly leading to two conference presentations and three manuscripts currently in preparation.
* **Collaboration:** Facilitated technical project management across the department, ensuring standardized data practices for large-scale neuronal datasets.
