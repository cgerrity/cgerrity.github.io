# Research & Engineering Projects

### Multi-Task Gated Recurrent VAE (MT-GR-VAE)
This model was engineered to process high-density neural recordings for multi-label, multi-class decision decoding.
* **Impact:** Achieved 80% accuracy on reward-associated predictions and 62% balanced accuracy across all labels.
* **Technical Detail:** Utilized adaptive loss normalization to decode cognitive states from the Prefrontal Cortex and Anterior Cingulate Cortex.

### Scalable Neural Processing Pipeline
I established a high-performance signal chain for acute high-density electrophysiology (300+ channels) integrating NeuroNexus probes and Intan controllers.
* **Performance:** Optimized the deep learning training pipeline to increase training speed 4x over the previous system.
* **Scale:** Built a scalable MATLAB-based pipeline that has processed over 4 TB of massive neurophysiological data to date.

### Explainable AI in Neuroscience
Using XAI techniques, I identified novel brain region contributions to learning by analyzing the neural network decoding model. This work bridges the gap between complex "black box" models and biological insights.
