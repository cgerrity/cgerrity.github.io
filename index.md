# Hello! I'm Charles Gerrity

I recently completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Vanderbilt University and am currently a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Department of Psychology. My research focuses on developing deep learning models for neural data analysis and cognitive brain-computer interfaces.

My doctoral dissertation in Electrical and Computer Engineering focused heavily on the theoretical underpinnings of neural decoding and fronto-striatal circuits. I specialize in processing large-scale neuronal datasets (over 2 TB) using advanced deep learning architectures for decoding choice and decision-making processes. 

## Skills & Expertise
* **Programming Languages:** MATLAB, Python (NumPy), R, LaTeX, Git
* **Deep Learning & AI:** Variational Autoencoders, LSTM & GRU Networks, Convolutional Neural Networks, ResNet
* **Neuroscience Tools:** High-density Electrophysiology, Intan Recording Systems, Open Ephys, NAN Microdrives, NeuroNexus Probes
* **Research Competencies:** Systems Neuroscience, Brain-Computer Interfaces, Animal Research, Big Data Processing

## Current Research & Projects

**MT-GR-VAE Development**
Engineered a Multi-Task Gated Recurrent Variational Autoencoder for processing high-density neural recordings, achieving multi-label, multi-class decision decoding.

**Cognitive State Decoding**
Utilized adaptive loss normalization to decode cognitive states from the Prefrontal Cortex and Anterior Cingulate Cortex.
